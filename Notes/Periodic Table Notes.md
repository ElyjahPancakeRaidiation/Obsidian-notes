#Class_Notes
##### **Main group (A column)**:
- 1A column - Alkaline metals:  **$1^+$** ion
- 2A column - Alkaline earth metals: **$2^+$** ion
- 3A column: **$3^+$** ion
- 4A column: None
- 5A column - Pnictogen: **$3^-$** ions
- 6A column - Chalcogens: **$2^-$** ions
- 7A column - Halogens: **$1^-$** ions

##### **Transition and post transition metals**
- These element can not be predicted by their column
- Cd - $2^+$
- Zn - $2^+$
- Ag - $1^+$
